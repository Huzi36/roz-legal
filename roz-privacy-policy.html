3. Location Data — Special Note

Roz requests access to your device's location to show you nearby stores and calculate delivery distances. We collect location data only:

  • When the app is in the foreground and you have granted location permission
  • At the time of opening the app to load nearby stores
  • When you place an order to calculate the delivery address

We do not track your location continuously, in the background, or after you close the app. You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings, though this will limit the app's ability to show nearby stores.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show you nearby stores relevant to your location
  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To send you order status notifications via push notification
  • To enable merchants to fulfil your orders (we share your delivery address with the merchant and delivery partner)
  • To process merchant payouts
  • To respond to your support queries
  • To detect and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build behavioural profiles for sale to third parties.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

5.1 Merchants

When you place an order, the merchant receives your delivery address, order details, and phone number to facilitate fulfilment. Merchants may not use this information for any purpose other than fulfilling your order.

5.2 Delivery Partners

Your delivery address and order details are shared with our third-party delivery partner (Borzo India Private Limited) to facilitate delivery. Borzo's own privacy policy governs their use of your data.

5.3 Supabase

Roz uses Supabase (a cloud database service) to store user data. Data is stored on servers in the Asia-Pacific region. Supabase processes data in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data to law enforcement, government authorities, or courts when required to do so by applicable Indian law, a court order, or legal process.

5.5 We Do Not Sell Your Data

Roz does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Order data is retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes
  • If you delete your account, personal data is deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Location data is not stored beyond the duration of your session

7. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA) and applicable Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Grievance Redressal — raise a complaint about how your data is handled
  • Withdrawal of Consent — withdraw consent for data processing at any time (this may affect your ability to use the Platform)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rozhyperlocal@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Roz is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete the account and associated data promptly.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access controls on our database, and secure storage through Supabase. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy
